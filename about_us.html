ONE LINE ABOUT OUR STORY

Our Story

TCF is for people on the go, people who are busy, love fashion, need help, you name it. It for everyone who’s ever thought ‘I wish I had a stylist.’

We want to share ideas on building and organizing your wardrobe in capsules. We would also like to address style issues that each of us have on a daily basis .

We use our expertise and personalized analysis and come up with looks which have a mix of high-end, high-street buys and new finds to quirk up your wardrobe. We also bring to life what's already in your closet. NO issue is too tough to tackle here , we’ve got you covered .

Welcome to TCF , lets get Styling !

Smruti

I am a trained apparel designer from London College of Fashion.

I have Work experience of over 15years where I have worked with exporters like Mandhana Industries , to brands like Chemistry India.

However, with the number of options available now I realize the bigger question is what to buy and how to style ! Always pressed for time ,as a working mother, I realized the need for a digital wardrobe to be able to present my best self at work as well as social events !

Aditi

I am a Chartered Accountant , Energy Healer and an advocate for body positivity.

I have dealt with body image issues for most part of my life and with the TCF I would like to empower women to embrace their present selves and be able to present the best version of themselves.

I realized a lot of women face three key issues – what to wear , how to wear and where to wear. That’s why have created a platform that will provide answers to these questions!
